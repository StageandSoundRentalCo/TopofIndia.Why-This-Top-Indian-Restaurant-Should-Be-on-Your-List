Why Top of India Is the Top Indian Restaurant

Indian food is known for its bold and vibrant flavors, and at Top of India we make it our mission to serve dishes that reflect the true essence of India’s diverse culinary landscape. Located at the heart of Spokane Valley, we take pride in providing an experience that goes beyond just a meal. Here’s why Top of Indiastands out as the top Indian restaurant in the region:

Authentic Indian Cuisine with a Local Touch

Indian food varies greatly from region to region, with each area of the country offering its own signature dishes, techniques, and flavors. At Top of India,, our chefs bring their expertise from various regions of India, ensuring that every dish is crafted with the utmost authenticity. From the creamy and rich curries of the North to the tangy and spicy flavors of the South, we offer an extensive menu that showcases the incredible diversity of Indian cuisine.

Whether you’re in the mood for a classic North Indian Butter Chicken or a spicy South Indian Dosa, Top of India ensures that every dish captures the true flavors of the region it represents. Our chefs use traditional cooking methods and locally sourced ingredients to make sure each meal is as close to the authentic taste of India as possible.

A Feast for the Senses

At Top of India, every dish is a feast for the senses. The first thing that greets you when you walk into our restaurant is the intoxicating aroma of spices wafting through the air. Indian cuisine is famous for its intricate use of spices, and we carefully balance each one to create dishes that are not only flavorful but also aromatic.

The colorful presentation of our dishes adds to the sensory experience. Whether it’s the golden hue of a rich curry or the vibrant green of fresh herbs, every dish is as visually appealing as it is delicious. Our food is designed to captivate your senses from the moment you enter the restaurant to the last bite.

Vegetarian, Vegan, and Gluten-Free Options for All

Indian cuisine is incredibly versatile, with many dishes that cater to different dietary preferences. At Top of India, we understand the importance of offering a diverse menu that accommodates everyone. Whether you are a vegetarian, vegan, or someone with dietary restrictions, we have something to suit your needs.

Indian vegetarian cuisine is known for its bold flavors and creative use of vegetables, lentils, and legumes. Some of our most popular vegetarian dishes include Chole Bhature, Aloo Gobi,, and Tandoori Paneer.. For our vegan guests, we offer a range of plant-based dishes that are as satisfying as they are flavorful, such as Baingan Bharta (roasted eggplant curry) and Dal Tadka (spiced lentils).

We also recognize the importance of catering to those with gluten sensitivities. Many Indian dishes are naturally gluten-free, and we offer several options on our menu that are perfect for anyone avoiding gluten, such as Rice Pilaf and Tandoori Shrimp..

Fresh, Quality Ingredients: The Secret to Great Indian Food

At Top of India, we believe that the foundation of great Indian food lies in the quality of the ingredients. Indian cuisine relies heavily on fresh herbs, spices, and vegetables, and we go to great lengths to ensure that only the finest ingredients are used in our dishes. From the selection of tender meats to the vibrant vegetables and aromatic spices, everything that goes into our food is carefully chosen to provide the best possible taste.

The spices used in Indian cooking are the heart and soul of the dishes, and we use freshly ground spices whenever possible to ensure maximum flavor and aroma. By sourcing our ingredients from trusted suppliers and local markets, we are able to maintain the authenticity and quality of our dishes, allowing our guests to experience the best of Indian cuisine.

While great food is at the core of Top of India, we also believe that a comfortable and inviting atmosphere is key to a memorable dining experience. Our restaurant is designed to provide a warm and welcoming environment where guests can relax and enjoy their meal in comfort.

The moment you step inside, you’ll feel right at home as you are greeted by our friendly and attentive staff, ready to make your experience exceptional.

Family-Friendly and Group Dining

Indian food is often best enjoyed in a group, as it encourages sharing and communal dining. At Top of India, we embrace this tradition and offer large portions that are perfect for sharing with family and friends. Whether you’re enjoying a meal with loved ones or hosting a celebration, our restaurant is ideal for group dining.

Our spacious dining area allows for a comfortable and enjoyable experience, even for larger parties. We offer family-style platters that bring together a variety of dishes, allowing everyone to sample different flavors and textures. From our rich, flavorful curries to our crispy, aromatic biryanis, there’s something to satisfy every palate.

The Perfect Blend of Traditional and Modern Indian Flavors

While we pride ourselves on staying true to the traditional roots of Indian cuisine, we also understand the importance of adapting to modern tastes. Our chefs continually experiment with new techniques and ingredients to create innovative dishes that blend the best of both worlds. This combination of traditional flavors and contemporary influences ensures that our menu stays fresh, exciting, and relevant to today’s diners.

Some of our unique fusion dishes include Tandoori Lamb Chops, which are marinated in a special blend of spices and cooked to perfection, and Fusion Biryani, a creative twist on the traditional rice dish. These fusion items give you a taste of India with a modern twist, making them perfect for those who love trying new things.

Signature Dishes You Won’t Want to Miss

At Top of India, we offer a wide variety of dishes that cater to all tastes. Some of our signature dishes that you won’t want to miss include:

  • Butter Chicken: A creamy, rich curry made with tender chicken, tomatoes, and a special blend of spices, creating a flavor that is both comforting and indulgent.
  • Lamb Rogan Josh: A traditional Kashmiri dish, this aromatic lamb curry is slow-cooked to perfection with spices like cardamom and cinnamon, offering a rich and flavorful experience.
  • Tandoori Chicken: Marinated in yogurt and spices, this dish is cooked in a traditional clay oven, creating a smoky, tender result that’s full of flavor.
  • Biryani: A fragrant rice dish made with basmati rice, meat, and a special blend of spices, biryani is a hearty and satisfying dish that has become a favorite worldwide.
